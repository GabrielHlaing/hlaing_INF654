Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

200g spaghetti, 100g pancetta, 2 large eggs, 50g Pecorino cheese, 50g Parmesan cheese, Freshly ground black pepper, Salt

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente (about 8 minutes).
  2. While pasta is cooking, heat pancetta in a pan over medium heat until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and both cheeses in a bowl, then season with black pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  5. Toss pasta in the pan with pancetta, then quickly combine with the egg mixture.
  6. Add pasta water to create a creamy sauce. Serve immediately.

Chicken Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

2 chicken breasts, 1 romaine lettuce, 50g Parmesan cheese, 2 slices of whole grain bread, Caesar dressing

Steps:

  1. Grill the chicken breasts until fully cooked, then slice.
  2. Toss chopped romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing.
  3. Add sliced chicken, crumbled toast, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Serve chilled, with additional dressing if desired.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

1 pizza dough, 200g mozzarella cheese, 2 ripe tomatoes, Fresh basil leaves, Olive oil, Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (475°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet.
  3. Layer mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and basil leaves on the dough.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

500g ground beef 12 taco shells, 1 onion, diced, 1 packet taco seasoning, 100g shredded cheddar cheese, (Salsa, guacamole, and sour cream) for topping

Steps:

  1. Brown ground beef with diced onion in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and cook according to package instructions.
  3. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  4. Fill each taco shell with seasoned beef, then top with cheese, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

4 slices of bread, 2 tablespoons butter, 4 slices of cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheese between two slices of bread, buttered side out.
  4. Grill the sandwich in the pan until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

1 cup broccoli florets, 1 red bell pepper, 1 carrot, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 clove garlic, Cooked rice for serving

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a wok or large pan.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, until tender.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.

Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

4 salmon fillets, 1 lemon, Fresh herbs, Olive oil, Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking tray.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, then season with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs.
  4. Top each fillet with a slice of lemon.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until salmon is cooked through.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 teaspoons baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 cup milk, 1 egg, 2 tablespoons butter

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
  2. In another bowl, mix together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour batter in 1/4 cup portions.
  5. Cook each pancake for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden brown.
